Sky Survey

Sky Survey is an augmented reality app that shows star map on iPad and iPhone. Sky Survey uses real-world photographic imagery of the starry sky as the primary component of the graphics. The app reacts to geographic location of the user and the 3D orientation of the mobile device. This way the user can position the device against a section of the sky and see the appropriate stars presented on the screen in the direction of his view. Interactive search option provides the ability to quickly locate stars, galaxies, and more. The user just needs to turn the mobile device following an arrow on the screen. If more info about an object is needed, within a touch of a finger, related Wikipedia article will be shown inside the app.

SecondPrism

SecondPrism mobile app provides a quick way to extract, filter, and visualize enterprise data as charts. The server-side allows companies to connect their data to the system and to be available for the mobile app. SecondPrism app has a large set of charting capabilities to choose from: starting from pie charts to 3D word clouds. In addition, the user can comment on a chart and share it with other users of the system.

9x9.tv

R&D Arts developed the mobile app for 9x9.tv company. The app is part of a global cloud TV platform provided by 9x9.tv that enables TV stations to curate their linear channels into non-linear and interactive channels broadcasted over Internet to viewers on multiple screens including smartphones and tablets. The mobile app has a control screen where a user can manage favorite channels. Hitting on a channel will open this channel with ability to switch between shows on this channel.

Deem Ground Driver

Deem Ground Driver is a mobile app for drivers that are being managed via a fleet management system with a centralized server-side support. Each driver can authenticate himself via a mobile device and get specific daily tasks from a centralized system. For each driving task, the driver gets detailed info, a navigation capability, an ability to summarize customer payments, and an ability to get customer’s signature on the mobile device. In addition, the driver can communicate with a dispatcher via a messaging system.

Video Editing Framework

We worked on the video editing functionality for a desktop application. The development was done for both Windows and MacOS. Developed functionality: insert/remove frames from the video, overlay of images and maps on top of a movie, stitching two movies together, splitting a movie into two parts.

R&D Arts map kit

Our internal framework. The user can cash an area of interest on his mobile device and crop an area out of a map. It is also possible to change map providers or even combine several map providers to enhance the amount of available data. The framework allows to provide image overlay and download overlays in KML format. In addition, we programmed the ability to visualize real-time moving vehicles on the map. Such ability was successfully used for fleet management software that we developed for one of our clients.

R&D Arts Plotter

Our internal framework for development of charts on mobile. Available charts: pie chart, scatter chart, line chart, bar chart; The framework provides an ability to zoom-in to see more details, to move a chart, to move a ruler on the chart, and to place markers on the charted area.
