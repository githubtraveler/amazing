SkySurvey

Sky Survey is an augmented reality app that shows start map on iPad and iPhone. Sky Survey uses real-world photographic imagery of the starry sky as the primary component of the graphics. The app reacts to geographic location of the user and the 3D orientation of the mobile device. This way the user can position the device against a section of the sky and see the appropriate stars presented on the screen in the direction of his view. Interactive search option provides the ability to quickly locate stars, galaxies, and more. The user just needs to turn the mobile device following an arrow on the screen. If more info about an object is needed, within a touch of a finger, related Wikipedia article will be shown inside the app.

SecondPrism

SecondPrism mobile app provides a quick way to extract, filter, and visualize enterprise data as charts. The server-side allows companies to connect their data to the system and to be available for the mobile app. SecondPrism app has a large set of charting capabilities to choose from: starting from pie charts to 3D word clouds. In addition, the user can comment on a chart and share it with other users of the system
